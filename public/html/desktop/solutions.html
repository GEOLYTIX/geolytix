Solutions

MAPP is our browser interface that brings all our knowledge and data directly into your hands.

Secure and available whenever and wherever you are, MAPP allows spatial intelligence to be available for every decision.

Our consultancy services allow us to optimise the way you access spatial intelligence from our latest geodata packages and your own business metrics.

Build on top of an open source API, the MAPP interface provides customisable data views.

Reports may be composed of data views which include layered maps, charts and tables.

Tiered security allows for the publication of open data as well as role based access to sensitive information.

Client specific models developed by our data scientists and associates may be integrated and evaluated on the fly through the MAPP browser interface.

Data and the API itself may be hosted on your premises or with a cloud services partner. Maintained either by yourself or our expert systems engineers.

Please register an account on our MAPP demo instance allowing our associates to contact you with additional information in regard to GEOLYTIX' analytics web solutions.
